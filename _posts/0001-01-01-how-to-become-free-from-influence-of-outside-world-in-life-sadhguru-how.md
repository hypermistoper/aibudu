{% raw %}How can one be free in life ? On other words how to achieve freedom from outside influences in life. Sadhguru in this video explains that one should learn to become blissful by himself or herself without the need of any outside situation,. Once this happens he or she can handle life without any problems even when they are lonely . If they are living their life as per their needs then they live a mediocre life . Whereas if one lives simply being blissed out and doing what is needed then they lead a magnificent life.{% endraw %}
