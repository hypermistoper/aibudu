Mostly stuck being a sidekick or limited to supporting roles, the onion rarely gets to enjoy the spotlight it truly deserves. To do our part and make sure that the onion gets to share all of its many layers with the world, we've come up with 6 recipes that bring the onion front and center! These delicious grilled onion bowls are stuffed to the brim with a whole range of different fillings and then grilled to absolute perfection. Teriyaki Chicken Onion Bomb<br /><br />You'll Need:<br /><br />- 1 large onion<br />- 2 oz chicken breast, chopped<br />- 1 scallion, chopped<br />- 1 garlic clove, minced<br />- 3 tbsp teriyaki sauce<br />- ½ tsp sesame seeds<br />- 1 pinch of sugar<br />- pepper<br /><br />* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *<br /><br />2. Philly Cheesesteak Onion Bomb<br /><br />You'll Need:<br /><br />- 1 large onion<br />- 2 oz steak bites<br />- salt &amp; pepper to taste<br />- 1 jalapeño, sliced<br />- ¼ red onion, chopped<br />- 1 slice of provolone<br /><br />* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *<br /><br />3. Caprese Onion Bomb<br /><br />You'll Need:<br /><br />- 1 large onion<br />- ½ tomato, chopped<br />- 1 tbsp pesto<br />- ¼ cup shredded mozzarella<br />- salt &amp; pepper to taste<br /><br />* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *<br /><br />4. Pork Tenderloin &amp; Pear Onion Bomb<br /><br />You'll Need:<br /><br />- 1 large onion<br />- 2 oz pork tenderloin, chopped<br />- salt &amp; pepper to taste<br />- ¼ pear, diced<br />- crumbled gorgonzola cheese<br /><br />* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *<br /><br />5. Onion Bomb Stuffed With Southern Baked Beans<br /><br />You'll Need:<br /><br />- 1 large onion<br />- thick-cut bacon, chopped<br />- ¼ cup baked beans<br />- sliced chili pepper<br />- salt &amp; pepper to taste<br /><br />* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *<br /><br />6. Italian Sausage &amp; Fennel Onion Bomb<br /><br />You'll Need:<br /><br />- 1 large onion<br />- ½ Italian sausage, sliced<br />- 1 mushroom, chopped<br />- chopped fennel<br />- sliced lemon<br />- salt &amp; pepper to taste<br /><br />* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *<br /><br />Here's How:<br /><br />Cut the top off of each large onion to create a small lid and hollow out the insides with a spoon. Fill the hollowed-out onions with the different fillings, place the lid on top of each onion, and transfer the onion bombs to a muffin pan. Position pieces of charcoal to the left and right sides of the grill, light the grill, and position the muffin pan in the center so that it's surrounded by hot charcoal. Cover the grill and cook the onion bombs for 20–30 minutes.<br /><br />Would you look at those onions! Grilling these onion bombs makes everything just a little bit sweeter and finally gives the onion a chance to shine. Using an onion as an edible container adds so many delicious, complex layers to to this dish!
