{% raw %}Homemade Lamborghini Aventador made of cardboard!<br />This is something I'v been wanted to do for around 2 years now but never found the time to do it! Every since I drove a Lambo in L.A the cars been stuck on my mind. I can't afford to buy one and nobody will rent a Lambo to a 22 year old so I'm stuck with one option... I need to make my own hahah! I found a sick paper model Lambo on <a rel="nofollow" target="blank" href="http://www.visualspicer.com">http://www.visualspicer.com</a> so I took that design but made it 5.6x bigger! I also wanted to make the car &quot;drive&quot;. Originally I was going to have my electic skateboard motor power that car but it turns out it wasn't really strong enough to move such a big thing so I opted for the next best thing... Fred Flintstone power!! By that I mean just walking with my feet on the inside hahah!<br />This whole project took around 2 and a half weeks to complete! making the cardboard body took around 1 week and the driving skeleton took 1.5 weeks! <br />Thats why I haven't been posting anything on Youtube lately haha! <br />Anyway,<br />Hope you enjoyed the video!
