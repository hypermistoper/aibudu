{% raw %}A struggling artist is faced with the reality that he has potentially failed in the attempt to make a living off of his art. After he receives an E-mail by an online art marketplace telling him that one of his paintings sold for 2700 dollars, he is caught in disbelieve at first but quickly understands that his life might finally change for the better. <br />Trying to paint a new canvas a while later he is not able to conjure up the creative spirits that would enable him to create art like the painting he recently sold online.
