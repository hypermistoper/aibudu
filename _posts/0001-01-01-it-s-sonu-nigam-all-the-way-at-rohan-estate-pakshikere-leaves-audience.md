{% raw %}Mangaluru: Over 5000 people gathered at Rohan Estate, Pakshikere on Saturday March 5 in the City outskirts were left captivated by the charm and of course mellifluous voice of celebrated playback singer Sonu Nigam. For the Sonu Nigam lovers the three-hour-wait as if did not matter, he was given a standing ovation on his arrival. His performance and rendition of variety of songs from 'Has mat pagli'  to 'Ye dil deewana' left audience only asking for more.
