{% raw %}In the Interim is a new networking webinar series hosted by Interim Dean Bruce Mueller. Each week, Michigan Pharmacy Students have the opportunity to connect with prominent alumni and faculty. <br /><br />Most recently, as Senior Director of Market Engagement for AscensionConnect, Brook DesRivieres was responsible for C-Suite and Regional Technology Officer relationships for Ascension Ministries in Oklahoma, Kansas, Wisconsin, and the National Enterprise Pharmacy Service Line.<br /><br />Prior to joining AscensionConnect, Dr. DesRivieres served in progressive pharmacy leadership roles culminating in the Metro-Milwaukee Regional Director of Pharmacy and Residency Program Director for their multi-site PGY2 in Health System Pharmacy Administration and Leadership at Ascension Wisconsin.<br /><br />Dr. DesRivieres is currently responding to a request from the Wisconsin Emergency Assistance Volunteer Registry as an immunizing pharmacist combating COVID-19.<br /><br />She obtained her Doctor of Pharmacy from the University of Michigan, Masters in Health-System Pharmacy from The Ohio State University and ASHP-accredited 24 month PGY1 residency training from Grant Medical Center, part of OhioHealth.{% endraw %}
