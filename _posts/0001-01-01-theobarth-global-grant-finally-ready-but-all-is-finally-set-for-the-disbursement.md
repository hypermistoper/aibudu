{% raw %}All is finally set for the disbursement of the Theobarth Global Foundation's International Grant to about fifty million Nigerians.<br /><br />The grant which is now at its final stage of processing is awaiting the green light from the Central Bank of Nigeria, CBN to be disbursed to the beneficiaries any time soon.<br /><br />The Country Director of the Foundation,Theophilus Oluche Ebonyi recently said the move is aimed at encouraging Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises in order to alleviate the masses' sufferings.<br /><br />He said the foundation has finalised plans with the relevant government authority to ensure that proper procedure is carried out before the smooth disbursement of the grant.<br /><br />Mr. Ebonyi also warned middle men on the danger of extorting the beneficiaries.<br /><br />The Country Director however, advised  the would be beneficiaries of the Grant to use it judiciously in to improve their standard of living and that of their family members.{% endraw %}
