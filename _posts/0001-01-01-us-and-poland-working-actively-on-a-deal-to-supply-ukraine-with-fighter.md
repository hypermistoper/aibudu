{% raw %}US Secretary of State Antony Blinken said Sunday that the United States was &quot;working actively&quot; on a deal with Poland to supply Ukraine with fighter jets. The deal, according to reports, could involve Poland handing over its existing MIG-29s, a Soviet/Russian-made jet fighter Ukrainian pilots are familiar with, to Ukraine. The US would then provide its F-16 fighters to Poland as replacements. US officials, including Blinken, had downplayed the possibility of any NATO member supplying besieged Ukraine since the beginning of this week.
