{% raw %}Extremely overcrowded Jam-packed rush hour Eid special train Rajshahi Express (Dhaka to Chapainawabganj) of Bangladesh Railway leaving Dhaka Airport Railway Station, Dhaka District, Bangladesh with thousands of Eid Passengers inside and outside of the train including the train roof.  <br />Please note: This is not a regular situation of Bangladesh Railway. Such an overcrowded situation arisen specially on the occasion of Eid festival, the main religious festival of Muslims of Bangladesh. Around 8 millions peoples leaves Dhaka city towards their home in the small cities and villages to celebrate Eid festival in the last 2 days before Eid. This is almost impossible to manage such a huge crowd for a small country like Bangladesh. This journey not brings happiness every time, in several occasion peoples lost their life for the cause of this dangerous journey.<br /><br />Camera Use for capturing this video: Sony AX 100E 4K Handycam{% endraw %}
