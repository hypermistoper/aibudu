I used 20 kg / 44lb and it ended up making around 4 l at 45%.  <br /><br />Process the peaches down into bite-sized bits.  <br />Just cover them with water. <br />Blits them up with a paint mixer or food processor stick. <br />Add pectic enzymes.  <br />Let sit for 4-12 hours to thin.<br />Blits them again with paint mixer or food processor stick.  <br />(optional) Increase gravity to 1.065 with sugar. <br />Pitch K1V116 yeast. <br />Leave to ferment until gravity stabilises near 1.00 <br />Strain the wash and perform stripping runs. <br />Spirit run again as a pot still (or perhaps use a couple of bubble plates)<br />Make good cuts!
