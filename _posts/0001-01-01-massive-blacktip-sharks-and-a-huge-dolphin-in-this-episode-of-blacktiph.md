Our first goal, as always, was to catch our live bait. Once we filled the tuna tubes, we headed offshore and started trolling. The first fish that showed up in the spread was a big dorado (Mahi-Mahi or Dolphin Fish). I tossed the belly bait, and the dorado ate it. After a difficult battle, we landed the dolphin, tagged and released it safely back to the ocean. Shortly after we started fishing on Zane Grey Reef and found some massive blacktip sharks!
