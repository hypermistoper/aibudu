{% raw %}This video details how I install a quick weave via the wig cap method using hair protector. The hair I'm installing is Zury Sis natural dream weave synthetic hair and can be found at most beauty supply stores. <br /><br /><br />Supplies Needed:<br />Hair<br />Hair glue<br />weave cap/ stocking cap<br />hair protectant or Got 2b glued hair spray<br />blowdryer <br />scissors<br />White eyeliner/lipliner pencil<br /><br /><br />Steps to install quick weave:<br /><br />PREP:<br />1. Braid down your hair (optional, can pull into ponytail/slick down), and make sure to leave out enough hair at the top to cover your tracks. <br />2. Put on the wig cap (any color)<br />3. Use a white eyeliner/lip liner pencil to mark the area around your leave out<br />3. Apply hair protectant to cap avoiding areas of leave out , can use gorilla snot hair spray or morning glory hair protectant<br />4. Blow dry hair protectant making sure it is COMPLETELY dry<br /><br />INSTALL<br />1. Measure the track hair against your head from ear to ear<br />2. Cut the weft that you measured<br />3. Apply hair glue to track and allow to dry for no longer than 30 seconds <br />4. Lay the track onto your cap beginning at the bottom working your way up<br />5. Repeat the above steps until you reach your leave out <br />6. cut off the remaining cap that's exposed (be careful!!)<br />7.
