{% raw %}Hey I am final year medical student in sri lanka. My name is Dulara piyumal. I started a YouTube channel 4 months ago as most of you are requested for more videos about medical student life. So this is it. Mediscenes with Dulara....<br />I am studying at Rajarata University medical faculty. We do our clinical appointments at teaching hospital Anuradhapura. That is the 3rd largest hospital in sri lanka. There are lot of doctors specialized in general medicine, surgery, pediatrics, Gyn and obs, psychiatry as well as lot of subspecialities. They guided us. <br />Our medical Faculty take 180 medical students for each year and they are undergone a 5 year long course to become a qualified doctor. At the end of the final year we all with all other medical students in sri lanka going to face common exam called FINAL MBBS to select for a internship. Before Our Final MBBS, the last year we have to stay in the professorial unit as a final year medical students. There we have to do five major appointments, each take 8 weeks. This is regarding our medicine appointments. <br />In our group there are 36 students. All are very funny. We enjoy our free time as  much as possible among our tough academic works.
