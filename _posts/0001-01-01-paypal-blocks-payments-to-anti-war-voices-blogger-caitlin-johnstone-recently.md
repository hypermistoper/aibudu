{% raw %}Blogger Caitlin Johnstone recently reported that online payment service PayPal had begun blocking payments to independent media sites known for promoting anti-war messages, particularly with regard to the Russia-Ukraine conflict. Caleb Maupin as well as MintPress News have been targeted by PayPal and told that they will no longer be able to receive funds from fans and supporters via the service.<br /><br />Jimmy and American comedian Kurt Metzger discuss this chilling development in the tech industry’s push to limit free speech and silence voices that counter the establishment message on key issues like war and peace.<br
