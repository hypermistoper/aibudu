I know that if I do all three I will be pleased looking back on my day. <br /><br />Since scheduling the time to read and write everyday I have seen improvement in my writing.. which makes me very happy! I'll never make the promise to become a great, divine, exquisite writer. I only promise to be A writer, setting free the stories in my head.
