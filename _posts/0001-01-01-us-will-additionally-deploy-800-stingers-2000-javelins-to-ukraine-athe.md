{% raw %}AThe United States will additionally deploy 800 Stingers, 2000 Javelins and 100 kamikaze drones to Ukraine.<br />The United States will soon transfer defense assistance to Ukraine for $1 billion.<br /><br />The United States of America decided to supply Ukraine with additional weapons and other military assistance in the amount of about $1 billion. If necessary, the United States is ready to increase the volume of military assistance to Ukraine, and the possibility of transferring long-range air defense systems to Ukraine is seriously considered.<br />At the moment, the information and news agency Avia.pro has information that, as military assistance, the United States intends to provide Ukraine with the following weapons and means of protection:<br /><br />• 800 anti-aircraft systems Stinger;<br /><br />• 2000 Javelins, 1000 light anti-armor weapons and 6000 AT-4 anti-armor systems;<br /><br />• 100 Switchblade 300 and Switchblade 600 tactical unmanned aerial systems;<br /><br />• 100 grenade launchers, 5000 rifles, 1000 pistols, 400 machine guns and 400 rifles;<br /><br />• More than 20 million small arms cartridges and grenade launchers and mortars;<br /><br />• 25 sets of body armor and 000 helmets.<br /><br />Experts note that the Stinger man-portable anti-aircraft missile systems, as well as tactical kamikaze drones, can pose a rather big threat, moreover, if the Switchblade 300 variant is designed to destroy unarmored vehicles and manpower, then the Switchblade 600 variant can already be used to defeat armored targets.<br /><br />Experts note that Russia is ready to respond to such actions by the United States, however, at the moment no official statements have been made in this regard.{% endraw %}
