{% raw %}Now available for the Reason Rack, the BV-X Multimode Vocoder is Reason Studios' modern take on a classic audio effect. Across nearly all musical genres from EDM, to hip hop, to pop and beyond vocoders have brought synthesizers to life. BV-X is available in the Reason Studios shop as a Rack Extension and is also included automatically with a Reason+ subscription.<br /><br />BV-X Multimode Vocoder is a new Effect that makes the classic vocoding sound accessible.<br />The built-in powerful synthesizer is custom-made for the vocoder and allows producers to get<br />a great result with just a few simple steps. Enabling Auto Play means you don’t have to play<br />any MIDI notes—BV-X will track along with your voice.<br /><br />BV-X Multimode Vocoder is available in the Reason Studios shop as a Rack Extension add-on for the Reason DAW and Reason Rack Plugin (VST, AU, AAX).{% endraw %}
