{% raw %}Multiple people were shot in a New York City subway station during rush hour on Tuesday, and a manhunt was underway for a suspect, officials said.<br /><br />At least 13 people were injured during the mayhem that unfolded at a station in Brooklyn’s Sunset Park neighborhood, the FDNY said, though it was not immediately clear how many of those victims were shot.<br /><br />A man, possibly wearing clothes that resembled MTA attire, was spotted throwing a device in the subway station before opening fire at about 8:30 a.m., law enforcement sources told NBC New York.<br /><br />The FDNY initially said several undetonated devices were found at the scene. The NYPD, though, said no “active explosive devices” were immediately located.
