{% raw %}What is afterski? Where is the best afterski in the world? Well, in a valley in Norway named Hemsedal you can find both of those things in one place. The unique concept of Afterski is the whole experience. It simply cant be explained by just showing the Party. In order to understand the Afterski concept you need to see the whole idea of traveling into the mountains, living at a cabin with friends, skiing the whole day before partying with your ski gear on until sundown.
