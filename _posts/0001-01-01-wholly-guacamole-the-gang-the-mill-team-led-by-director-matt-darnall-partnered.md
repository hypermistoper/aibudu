{% raw %}The Mill+ team, led by director Matt Darnall, partnered with BBDO San Francisco to bring the endearing and funny gang of Wholly Guacamole avocados to life. Darnall worked with the agency from the early stages of the process to conceptualize, create and translate to screen each of these tricky avocados' personalities. In addition, the Mill+ team developed a bespoke, hybrid Design and CG pipeline to allow enhanced versatility in the development of the five photo-real characters. The result is full of vitality and truly memorable.
