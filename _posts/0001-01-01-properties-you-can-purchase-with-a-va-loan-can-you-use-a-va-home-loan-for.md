{% raw %}Can you use a VA home loan for a vacation rental, condo, modular home or to build a house? It depends. Veterans, service members and their families can use VA loans for all sorts of properties but under certain guidelines. <br /> <br />The residence being purchased must become the full-time home of the borrower. That rules out some homes like investment properties, and it can be tough to find a VA lender willing to lend you money to construct a home.
