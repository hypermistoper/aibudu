{% raw %}If Registered Person is supplying Taxable as well as Exempt Supplies then he has to take Input Tax Credit proportionately.<br /><br />As per Section 17 (1) &amp; Section 17 (2) read with Rule 42 of CGST Rules 2017, such Input Tax Credit is to be taken in ratio of Turnover of each month.<br /><br />Every registered person is required to reverse Input Tax Credit out of Common I.T.C (Used for Taxable &amp; Exempt Supplies) proportionately in ratio of turnover of each month.<br /><br />Also at year end, such registered person is required to do annual working and avail or reverse such Input Tax Credit.<br /><br />Exempt Services are given in &quot;Notification No 12/2017, Central Tax dated 28/06/2017.<br /><br />Exempted Goods are given in &quot;Notification No 02/2017, Central Tax dated 28/06/2017.{% endraw %}
