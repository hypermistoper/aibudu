{% raw %}The horrific atrocities that took place in the Congo Free State are often compared to the Holocaust, with king Leopold II of Belgium  being compared to the Nazi leader Adolf  Hitler. Between 1885 and 1908, Leopold II personally ruled the Congo, in sub Saharan Africa, which was largely unexplored at this time, as only the coastal areas had been claimed by other European countries.
