{% raw %}Before deciding to invest in a mutual fund, you must first understand the types of mutual funds available in the capital market. Here are some types of mutual funds that you can choose:<br />1. Money Market Mutual Funds<br />In money market mutual funds, all investors' money will be put into money market instruments such as Bank Certification and bonds.<br />2. Fixed Income Mutual Funds<br />Fixed income mutual funds are a type of mutual fund where the investment funds are allocated to bonds in the amount of at least 80%. <br />3. Equity Funds<br />This type will enter most of the funds held in the stock market. The investment manager will put your funds in stocks that have the potential to provide profits.<br />4. Mixed Mutual Funds<br />This mutual fund places investors' funds into several investment instruments such as bonds, stocks, and deposits. This system will diversify the investment you make so that it has the potential to provide greater profits and less risk than just one instrument.<br />5. Protected Mutual Funds<br />This type of mutual fund will give you a 100% guarantee of your principal investment as long as your disbursement is done as agreed at the beginning.{% endraw %}
