{% raw %}Of Rest And Ritual is track five of, Metalcore band, Whisper Dying's debut EP Terror In The Eyes Of The Villain. Terror In The Eyes Of The Villain was recorded in late 2006 and released on May 16th 2007. The EP has a full running time of 25.2 minutes of chugging riffs  and heavy breakdowns accompanied by melodic lyrics. Terror In The Eyes Of The Villain was self released by Whisper Dying. Whisper Dying was formed in Orlando, Florida, active from 2004 - 2010. The last show Whisper Dying put on was on April 16th, 2010 at Liquid Scrips Bar, thus leaving them in an official permanent hiatus period as of now. Whisper Dying has achieved some major success sharing the stage with acts such as A Day To Remember,  Light This City, Bullet For My Valentine, Sevendust, Nonpoint, Papa Roach and Chevelle. As well as playing as an opening act at Orlando's Real Rock 101.1's popular Earthday Birthday 14. Terror In The Eyes Of The Villain is a dope EP and a good listen for those hardcore fans out there looking for a band that brings some major metalcore music with a sound inspired by progressive metalcore band Misery Signals.
