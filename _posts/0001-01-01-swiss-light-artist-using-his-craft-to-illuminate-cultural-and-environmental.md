The projection of a tiger image more than 5 kilometers long and over 2 kilometers tall is a nod to the Chinese lunar calendar and international solidarity. Hofstetter timed the light art to coincide with the start of the Beijing Winter Olympics as a beacon of motivation for athletes and a moment to drum up environmental awareness globally.
