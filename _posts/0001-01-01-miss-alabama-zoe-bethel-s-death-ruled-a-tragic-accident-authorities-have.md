{% raw %}Authorities have ruled the death of Zoe Sozo Bethel an accident. Bethel, who was crowned Miss Alabama for America Strong and was a conservative political commentator, fell from a third-floor window, according to the Miami Police Department. They say officers &quot;responded to a call of a possible suicide attempt,&quot; and that the Miami Dade Medical Examiner ruled her death was caused by &quot;blunt force trauma.&quot; Police say &quot;This was a tragic accident, and no foul play is suspected.&quot;{% endraw %}
