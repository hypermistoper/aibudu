{% raw %}#GalaxyS22Ultra #Samsung #SamsungUnpacked<br /><br />The Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra in India comes with top-of-the-line features and specs. It retains the best bits of the retired Galaxy Note series - the sleek flattened design, the extra-large display and the iconic S Pen. Galaxy S22 Ultra features the latest Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 chipset with12GB RAM and up to 512GB storage. The Galaxy S22 Ultra display is a 6.8-inch QHD+ Dynamic AMOLED 2X display, with a variable refresh rate up to 120Hz. The display has a peak brightness of up to 1750 nits. S22 Ultra camera specs include a 108MP main wide camera, 12MP ultra-wide camera, and two 10MP telephoto cameras with 3x and 10x optical zoom respectively. Galaxy S22 Ultra battery is a 5,000mAh unit with 45W wired fast charging support. The phone also gets 15W wireless charging, and Wireless PowerShare which allows it to charge other devices. The Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra runs OneUI 4.1, based on Android 12, out of the box. It is IP68 rated for dust and water resistance.
